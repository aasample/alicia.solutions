Feature Spotlight: QR Code Upload

The innovation I'm most proud of

The Challenge: Users need to photograph items throughout their home, but managing them on desktop is easier. Traditional file uploads are clunky on mobile.

The Solution: Desktop generates a secure QR code → User scans with phone → Opens mobile-optimized upload page → Photos sync instantly to Firestore → Appear in desktop inventory in real-time.

Why It Matters: This cross-device workflow shows practical UX problem-solving and cloud architecture thinking - exactly what agentic AI companies need.
